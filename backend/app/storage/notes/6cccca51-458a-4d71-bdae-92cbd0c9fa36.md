### Task Summary: Enterprise AI Agent Adoption Patterns

**Executive Conclusion**
Enterprise adoption of AI agents is currently concentrated in **Finance, Insurance, Human Resources, and Customer Experience**. High-value workflows being automated include **claims processing, supply chain optimization, autonomous recruiting, and personalized customer support**. While vendors report substantial ROI and production-ready deployments, specific quantitative performance metrics remain unverified in the available evidence.

In banking, examples include fintech firms using agents to initiate transfers and optimize savings based on real-time spending patterns [8allocate].
