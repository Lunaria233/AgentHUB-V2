**Key Conclusions**
Enterprise AI agent adoption is currently constrained primarily by security governance and data integration complexity, rather than model capability alone. While agents are shifting toward autonomous workflow execution (ERP, CRM, cybersecurity), the provided evidence lacks specific named company deployment case studies. Success factors identified include unified data access, role-based security controls, and continuous feedback loops.

**Adoption Barriers**
*   **Security & Governance:** Cited as the top challenge by 53% of leadership and 62% of practitioners (Architecture & Governance, Jan 2025). Compliance frameworks and audit processes are mandatory before production deployment.
*   **Data Integration:** 42% of enterprises require access to eight or more data sources to deploy agents effectively. Fragmented data access and scaling complexity are recurring technical bottlenecks.
*   **Human-AI Collaboration:** Organizations must establish clear frameworks to balance agent autonomy with human oversight.

**Deployment Scope & Technical Requirements**
*   **Use Cases:** Reported applications include risk governance, cybersecurity, enterprise search, and customer experience. Agents are expected to orchestrate workflows and execute tasks aligned with business objectives.
*   **Infrastructure:** Implementation requires connecting agents to over 100 business systems via pre-built connectors handling authentication and schema translation.
*   **Operational Controls:** Robust security features (data encryption, authentication) and observability tools are necessary to monitor agent decisions and behaviors.

**Evidence Limitations**
*   **Deployment Specificity:** The retrieved search results describe industry trends and architectural recommendations but do not provide specific real-world examples of named enterprises or quantified ROI from actual deployments.
*   **Source Context:** Insights are drawn from industry councils and vendor-adjacent publications (Forbes, Datagrid, Xenonstack, Kore.ai), which may emphasize strategic positioning over independent empirical data.
*   **Timeline:** Most projections reference trends extending through 2025, suggesting the technology is in an early-to-mid adoption phase.
